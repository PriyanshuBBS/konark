#Know Us

At Konark Fire Solutions, our mission is to protect material and property by providing top-notch fire safety & Plumbing solutions tailored to the unique needs of our clients.

Our Story
Founded in 1995, Konark Fire Solutions was born out of a vision to make the industry a safer place. With a team of dedicated professionals who are passionate about fire safety & plumbing solutions, we embarked on a journey to deliver comprehensive fire prevention, detection, and suppression solutions to businesses, homeowners, and institutions.we are driven by a passion for safety and a dedication to our clients. We take pride in playing a crucial role in safeguarding lives and property. When you choose us, you choose a partner who is always there to protect what matters most.
Fire Safety System

Safety from fire is one of the biggest concerns for any developer or promoter. Not only it poses a direct threat on hundreds of lives but can also damage the property beyond repairs. At Konark Fire Solutions, we understand that very well.

Sanitary and Plumbing Works

Every housing or business project comes with its own water treatment requirements. Water is an essential and valuable commodity and must be handled properly. We understand that and provide a complete bouquet of sanitary and plumbing services for all types of projects.
